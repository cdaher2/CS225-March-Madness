Welcome to the March Madness Bracket Simulator
To use this program:

First, enter the view you wish to edit by clicking on the appropriate region
Once in the region, click on the team you expect will win their matchup
Right-clicking on a team will bring up a window with auxiliary data
Once you have filled out the bracket, click on Finalize, followed by Simulate
You will then be presented with a list of scores, as well as the final simulated bracket, both of which can be accessed by clicking on the appropriate button at the top of the window
